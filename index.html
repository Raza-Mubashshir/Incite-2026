ABOUT InCITe

InCITe is a acronym for International Conference on Information Technology. After 4 successful editions, we bring to you the 5th edition of InCITe 2026 on the theme Agentic Intelligence: Empower your World. Agentic Intelligence is an emerging field of artificial intelligence that goes beyond automation to set goals, plan and execute tasks with minimal human intervention. With ongoing progress, It is expected to transform sectors like healthcare, education, and business by enabling intelligent automation, adaptive decision-making, and new forms of human-machine partnership. It enables systems not only to perceive, understand, and decide but also to take purposeful actions toward achieving goals. With rapid advancements, The purpose of InCITe 2026 is to provide a platform to explore the applications, challenges, and transformative impact of Agentic Intelligence.
